Questions people usually ask first

A few quick answers to help frame the right next step.

Do you handle hosting and domain setup?

Yes. Hosting, domain connection, analytics, and launch setup can all be handled so you are not piecing together the technical side alone.

Do I own the website and code?

Yes. We build in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and if you want the site files, we provide them. No gatekeeping.

What kind of business is the Base Site best for?

It is a strong fit for restaurants, clinics, contractors, and service businesses that need a clean static site with a straightforward inquiry path.

When do I need a custom build?

If the site needs user logins, dashboards, storage-heavy functionality, advanced integrations, or custom logic, that usually belongs in a custom scope.
